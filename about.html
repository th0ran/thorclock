Compass 2013
Industry Day
Monday 28th October 2013


Industry Day takes place on Monday 28th October 2013. Brought to you by Rave Plus and most importantly run by Ravensbourne Level 2 Students. Throughout the whole day from 4pm until 7pm Rave Plus has invited an impressive line up of key speakers from industry. Each speaker will be giving an invaluable insight into their career to date, they will explain to students and graduates how they made it to where they are today. All courses will have a speaker that is relevant to their specific industry sector and this year for the first time we will be live streaming selected speakers seminars world wide though the Horizon 2013 web site.
